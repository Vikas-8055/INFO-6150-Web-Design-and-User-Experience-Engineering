FAQ'S

What types of apartments are available?

We offer various types of apartments including Studios, 1 Bedroom, 2 Bedroom, and 3 Bedroom apartments. Each type comes with different sizes and rent prices to suit your needs.

How can I schedule a viewing?

You can schedule a viewing by contacting us through the "Contact Us" form on our website, or by calling us directly at 617-398-6945. Our team will assist you in setting up an appointment.

What is the process for applying for an apartment?

To apply for an apartment, fill out the application form on our website, provide necessary documents, and submit them for review. Our team will get back to you with further instructions.

Do you offer any special discounts or promotions?

We occasionally offer special discounts and promotions. Keep an eye on our website and sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on any current offers.
